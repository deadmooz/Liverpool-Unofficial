Stadium Tour

SEE ALL FOUR TROPHIES TOGETHER FROM MONDAY 21ST SEPTEMBER 2020

From Monday 21st September, you'll have a limited, once in a lifetime opportunity to have your photo taken with all four trophies together - Premier League, 18/19 UEFA Champions League, UEFA Super Cup, FIFA Club World Cup - and see our new museum exhibition The Boom Room when you book onto a Stadium Tour, Anfield Origins Tour or visit to The Liverpool FC Story Museum.

The Trophy Exhibition is a paid-for ticketed exhibition to help us comply with our social distancing and industry guidelines and so we can provide you with a safe and enjoyable visit.

To see the Trophy Exhibition book one of the available tours below.

To allow everyone time with the trophies at a safe distance, all visits must be pre-booked and there will be time limitations with the trophies for photo opportunities per party.  We kindly ask you not to touch the trophies to help us keep it safe for everyone.

ALL VISITORS MUST WEAR A MASK OR FACE COVERING AT ALL TIMES

The Boot Room Sports Cafe will remain temporarily closed, with snacks and drinks available to takeaway from one of the food kiosks located next to the museum by The Kop - card payments only. 

We understand that things might feel a little different next time you visit, however we look forward to welcoming fans and visitors back to Anfield Stadium very soon.
