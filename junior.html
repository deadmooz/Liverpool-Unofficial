LFC Junior Academy

Around the world our LFC International Academies provide young players with an opportunity to develop, learning new techniques and skills, all under the guidance of our coaching staff.

Global Talent Search

We offer something different and something unique by developing players both on and off the pitch, educating them through experiences that will shape their progression into young adults and giving them skills that can be used throughout their lives. Our overall aims are to bring LFC to our young fans around the world and to demonstrate just how special our football club is. Wherever you are lucky enough to attend a LFC coaching programme we hope you have fun and enjoy learning how to play ‘The Liverpool Way’

Academy Courses

Our LFC International Academy Soccer Schools consist of a variety of programmes delivered from the LFC Academy facility in Kirkby throughout the UK school holidays. The Liverpool FC Academy has set a benchmark for the development of young players both on and off the pitch. The 56 acre facility is host to some of the finest sporting and educational facilities in the country achieving Category One status in the Elite Player Performance Plan (EPPP) system. Education in all areas of the game is provided through various programme types and up to 100 children can attend per course. Each player will be coached in a variety of technical skills such as passing, receiving, dribbling, running with the ball and shooting in a way that recognises the ability and needs of each individual.
